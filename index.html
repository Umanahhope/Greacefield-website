Gracefield International Academy is a purpose-driven institution committed to nurturing young minds through academic excellence, moral integrity, and innovative learning. Academics : We Offer

We provide a complete academic journey from Creche to Secondary School, enriched with structured learning, character building, and hands-on STEM and ICT programs—nurturing every child's growth, creativity, and excellence.

Creche

Our Creche provides a warm, nurturing environment where babies and toddlers grow through play, care, and early learning support tailored to their developmental needs.

Pre-Nursery

We offer structured learning experiences that enhance early literacy, numeracy, and social skills, preparing little learners for a smooth transition into formal education.

Nursery

Our Nursery program fosters creativity, curiosity, and confidence through hands-on activities, interactive storytelling, and guided play in a safe and vibrant space.

Primary

The Primary School combines academic excellence with moral and character development, equipping pupils with the foundation for lifelong learning and leadership.

Secondary

Our Secondary School delivers a well-rounded curriculum designed to prepare students for higher education, global opportunities, and responsible citizenship.

Extracurricular Activities
& STEM/ICT Program

Through engaging activities like coding, music, arts, and sports, our STEM and extracurricular programs inspire creativity, teamwork, and real-life problem-solving.
